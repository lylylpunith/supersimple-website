Major Medicines for Daily Life

1. Aspirin

Aspirin is a common over-the-counter medication used to relieve pain and reduce inflammation. It is often used to treat headaches, muscle aches, and fever. Aspirin is also known for its blood-thinning properties, making it suitable for preventing heart attacks and strokes.

2. Paracetamol (Acetaminophen)

Paracetamol, also known as acetaminophen, is a widely used pain reliever and fever reducer. It is commonly used to alleviate mild to moderate pain associated with conditions such as headaches, toothaches, and muscle aches. Unlike aspirin, paracetamol does not have anti-inflammatory effects.

3. Ibuprofen

Ibuprofen is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) that is used to reduce pain, inflammation, and fever. It is often recommended for conditions such as arthritis, menstrual cramps, and injuries. Ibuprofen is known for its anti-inflammatory properties and is available over-the-counter in various formulations.
